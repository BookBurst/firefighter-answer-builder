THE INTERVIEW ANSWER BUILDER

Use this 1-page blueprint for EVERY question about your experience. Print a copy for each story you need to prepare.

STEP 1: DRAFT YOUR KEY POINTS

Break down your story into these 4 essential parts. Use bullet points or short notes.

THE SITUATION (Context):

THE CHALLENGE (Obstacle):

MY ACTION (What I did):

THE LESSON (The "So What?"):

STEP 2: BUILD YOUR WINNING ANSWER

Now, connect your key points from Step 1 into a single, powerful answer. Write it out exactly as you would say it.
